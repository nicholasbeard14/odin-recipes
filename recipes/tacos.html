Ground Beef Tacos

Description

These are the easiest tacos to make, and yet they always taste delicious. They should be a staple for all households.

The recipe below contains the bare necessities for the tacos. Add garnishes such as avocado, sour cream, salsa, lettuce, tomato, etc. for optimal taste.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Pam Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Spray pan with Pam spray, and heat the tortillas on the pan
  2. Cook ground beef on stove until brown and cooked through
  3. Follow the instructions on the taco seasoning packet to mix in with meat
  4. Serve
